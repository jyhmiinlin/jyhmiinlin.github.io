Dr Jyh-Miin Lin, MD, MSc, PhD

Physician-scientist

Jyh-Miin's interests include iterative reconstruction of the PROPELLER (Periodically Rotated Overlapping Parallel Lines with Enhanced Reconstruction) technique and spatio-temporal sparse reconstruction. Jyh-Miin's current project focuses on clinical applications of real-time reconstruction, using high-performance computing and graphic processing units (GPUs). 

Jyh-Miin was awarded a PhD from the University of Cambridge in July 2016. Before joining the Department of Radiology in 2012, Jyh-Miin obtained an MD from the Department of Medicine, National Taiwan University, in 2005. From 2005-2007, he worked at the magnetic resonance spectroscopy (MRS) lab in the Department of Electrical Engineering, National Taiwan University. 

Full List of Publications can be found in my <a href="https://orcid.org/0000-0002-2028-2899">ORCID</a> page.
